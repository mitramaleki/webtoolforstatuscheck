About this tool

This tool uses Claude claude-sonnet-4-6 with web search to research the current status of companies. For each company it:

  • Searches for the company's current operational status
  • Checks if the company has been acquired, merged, or closed
  • Finds the new URL if the company rebranded or changed domain
  • Classifies the country of origin
  • Determines if the company is a recruitment / poster / headhunter firm
